Sales analytics

Sales Performance Dashboard

Business question

How are sales distributed across branches, product categories, and payment methods?

What I did

I prepared retail sales records, defined the core performance measures, and created an interactive report for comparing branches, category mix, and customer payment choices.

What the data showed
  • 1,000 invoices generated 323K in sales, with an average invoice value of 323.
  • Branch C led sales at 111K, while Branches A and B were close at 106K each.
  • Category share was nearly even, and payment methods ranged narrowly from 31% to 35%.
Decision value

Provides one consistent view of sales performance and makes small differences between branches and customer choices easier to monitor.

Workforce analytics

HR Analytics Dashboard

Business question

What does workforce distribution, performance, attendance, and turnover look like across the organization?

What I did

I cleaned and grouped workforce records, compared departments, and designed a concise KPI view for staffing, attendance, performance, and reasons for leaving.

What the data showed
  • The report summarizes 75 employees, a 91% attendance rate, and 20% turnover.
  • Sales had the largest headcount at 12, while Purchasing had the smallest at 5.
  • IT and Marketing led average performance at 3.6, and resignation was the most common reason for leaving.
Decision value

Brings workforce allocation, attendance, performance, and turnover reasons into one view for a more focused HR review.

Financial analytics

Financial Analysis Dashboard

Business question

Which branches generate the most profit, and where are operating costs concentrated?

What I did

I consolidated revenue, expense, profitability, and cash-flow measures into one reporting view for comparing six branches and key cost categories.

What the data showed
  • Riyadh was the top-performing branch by net profit across all six locations.
  • Salaries and benefits represented about 42% of operating expenses, the largest cost category.
  • Electronics led category revenue, followed by Fashion and Home & Living.
Decision value

Creates a consistent view for comparing branch profitability, monitoring cash flow, and reviewing the largest cost drivers.
